Interaction Lab @ POSTECH

The Interaction Lab is an interdisciplinary research group within the Graduate School of Artificial Intelligence and the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at POSTECH.

We create new sensing and display technologies that foster powerful and immersive interactions between humans and computers. Our research efforts primarily lie in emerging modalities, including haptics, virtual/augmented reality (VR/AR), and multimodal interfaces, aiming to build the next generation of interactive systems.
